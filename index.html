Himachal Pradesh
Spiti Valley

Nestled at 12,500 ft, Spiti is India's last frontier — a lunar landscape of stark beauty. Ancient monasteries like Key and Tabo sit atop ridgelines, while the crisp mountain air carries the scent of juniper and history.

Assam
Majuli Island

The world's largest river island floats in the Brahmaputra, a living museum of Assamese culture. Watch traditional mask-makers at work in sattras (monasteries) and cycle through paddy fields at golden hour.

🗓 Oct – Mar 🎭 Cultural

West Bengal
Sandakphu Trek

At 11,941 ft, Sandakphu is the only spot in India where you can see Everest, Kangchenjunga, Lhotse, and Makalu together. The trek through Singalila National Park rewards you with rhododendron forests and cloud-ocean sunrises.

🗓 Apr – Jun, Oct – Dec 🏔 Trek
From ₹12,499 per person
Book Now
Karnataka
Gokarna

A sacred temple town that guards beaches no tourist map has fully tamed. Hike the coastal trail between Om Beach and Half Moon Beach for clifftop vistas, or kayak to secret coves accessible only by sea at low tide.

From ₹8,499 per person

Nagaland
Dzukou Valley

Dubbed the "Valley of Flowers of the Northeast," Dzukou blooms from June to September with rare lily species found nowhere else on Earth. The 6 km trek rewards with a hidden valley so serene it feels like another dimension.

🗓 Jun – Sep 🌸 Nature
From ₹11,999 per person
Book Now
Himachal Pradesh
Tirthan Valley

The Tirthan river carves through apple orchards and cedar forests on the edge of Great Himalayan National Park. Fly-fish for trout, trek to rainbow waterfalls, and sleep in heritage homestays with fireside storytelling.
